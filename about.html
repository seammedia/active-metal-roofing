Who We Are

Active Metal Roofing Vic Pty Ltd is a privately owned Australian business established in 2015, specializing in metal roof solutions for commercial and industrial spaces across Melbourne and Victoria.

We believe that a roof is not just a structure; it's the crown of your building. Our commitment to durability, protection, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

We specialize in a comprehensive range of commercial and industrial roofing services:

  • Commercial roofing installations and replacements
  • Architectural cladding for enhanced aesthetics
  • Re-roofing projects of any size
  • Industrial roofing for heavy-duty applications
  • Comprehensive roof inspections and assessments
