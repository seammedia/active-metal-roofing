Our Re-Roofing Process

1. Free Inspection & Assessment - We comprehensively evaluate your existing roof condition and provide honest recommendations.

2. Detailed Quote - Transparent pricing with no hidden costs. We explain all work required.

3. Planning & Scheduling - We work around your business operations to minimize disruption.

4. Professional Installation - Licensed plumbers using premium materials and proven techniques.

5. Quality Assurance - Thorough inspection and cleanup. We don't leave until you're satisfied.

Why Choose Active Metal Roofing?

With 22 years of combined experience and established since 2015, Active Metal Roofing brings proven expertise to every commercial re-roofing project. Our team consists entirely of licensed, registered plumbers who adhere to strict OH&S requirements.

We're committed to environmentally conscious practices, using sustainable and reusable materials. Our focus on efficient project completion means we respect your time and meet all agreed deadlines.
